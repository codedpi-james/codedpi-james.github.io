Our digital on-board marketplace: an essential response to COVID-19

Since 2019, Gate Group and Black Swan have been working together to develop a new digital retail platform called, currently called ‘ePAX’, for direct use by passengers in the cabin, during flight.

We understand that COVID-19 has left airlines in a challenging place, which is why we're now launching ePAX as a new zero-cost trial; offering hygiene-friendly onboard digital retail, that will help our partners improve the safety of the in-flight shopping experience and boost their revenues.

We look to the future, to recovery, and we see technology as a key part of this: both in terms of ensuring crew and passengers feel safer when they fly, and by enabling - or even enhancing - services that people enjoy when they travel. Our ultimate mission being to provide passengers and businesses the greatest choice, control and convenience throughout the travel experience.

As you can see, we’re still working on our website as we continue to roll out our products and services. If you have any questions or you’d like to get in touch, please contact us on partnerships@blackswan.com.
